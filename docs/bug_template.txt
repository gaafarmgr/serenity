# Platform

# Descripion of Issue
Remember that those reviewing your bug do not have all the information you
have about your bug.  Please be as detailed as possible in describing your bug
if you actually want it to be fixed.  Don't leave out details because you think
they are obvious or assume we know what you're talking about.  Be prepared to 
provide additional information if requested.
